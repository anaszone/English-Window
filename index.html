Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
Start with simple words and phrases. Search words in the dictionary daily, read their meanings and examples, and practice speaking, listening, reading, and writing a little every day.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes, this website is completely free to use. You can access all the dictionary features, search for words, and explore meanings without any cost.
3. Do I need to create an account?
No, you don’t need an account to use the basic features. You can search words and read their meanings freely. Creating an account is optional and only needed if you want to save your favorite words or track your learning progress.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
Search and learn new words daily using the dictionary. Read example sentences, practice speaking and writing them, and review your saved or favorite words regularly to remember them better.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
No, this website does not provide certificates. It focuses on helping you learn and improve your English vocabulary through practice and daily usage.
